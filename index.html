#1 About me

I hold a degree in law and my background in general is in business development with 20 years of experience. I worked for a well-known company in Estonia for a bit over 20 years, helping them to expand their business and grow. My main focus was developing their webshop.

20 years is a very long time to work for one company. I held various positions in that company which made it possible for me to grow in my job but at some point I felt I had reached the top and needed a career change. After a long consideration I decided to quit my job in May 2020 and make a transition into a new career as a software developer.

#2 The road to coding

It was in 2018, when, by accident, I stumbled upon this free Estonian MOOC (Massive Open Online Course) on Python programming "Intro to Python" and signed up. Already in the beginning of the course I was fascinated by what you can do with just a few lines of code. This sparked my interest! The course had 3 modules and in the end of 2019, after finishing the last module, I realized that this is something I need to explore in more detail and take my Python coding studies even further.

After quitting my job in May 2020 I dedicated myself completely to studying programming. I had no idea where this new journey would take me but inside I felt that this was, indeed, the right decision.

#3 Finding Treehouse

There are tons of online learning platforms out there to choose from when it comes to programming. How do you pick the one that suits you the most? I did a lot of research on the web and I found Treehouse.

What I like the most is that they have a really clear structure to learning different topics. All courses are highly interactive which means you'll have workshops, quizzes, practice sessions and code challenges regularly following each lesson in a course. You'll start building projects from day one.
